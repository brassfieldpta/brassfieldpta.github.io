Brassfield Parents and Teachers,

Our Memberhub PTA online dues processing system is open for business, so anyone can join the PTA on line in your pjs! Please join us on MemberHub where a slight fee is charged (total with fee, $10.86). You may also send in your membership form  to the PTA mailbox in the lobby.

We are having a membership drive and will celebrate 400 memberships with all the kids at a recess Popcorn Party, so please encourage spouses and family members to join!

  • At this point, only one membership can be processed at a time and each person must have either a unique phone number or email associated with their membership. So, if spouses have typically used one set of contact information, you must use a unique email for your membership and unique phone for your spouse and not use the same information in both membership forms.

Thank you so much for your support, we look forward to serving the kids this year, and hope you will join our team!

Tania Talbot
BRES PTA President, 2017-18
